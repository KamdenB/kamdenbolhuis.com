About Me

I first became interested in programming in 2014 when I was just 11. I started off with simple Lego Mindstorms programming, and gradually progressed on to Java, and later on to Web Development. It was in 2016 when I took a class at BizStream Academy that I knew I was hooked. I had the opportunity to stay on at BizStream for a private study mentorship for 8 months where I grew my ability even more. Since that time at BizStream, I have continued to progress my skills. I have decided that I am definitely interested in a career in Web Development.

Logicraft Industrial Systems

Freelance Android App Developer

Throughout the 2018/2019 school year I was hired to develop a signature app. During the development process, I learned a lot about managing changing client expectations and scope creep. The completed project was an app used to collect client signatures, upload them to google drive, and then insert them into a specific google sheet document. This app was built using the Ionic framework and custom GScripts to manage where the signature was put.
